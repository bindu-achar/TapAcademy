Tap Academy is an Indian ed-tech platform that focuses on technical skills development, particularly for students and professionals looking to break into the IT industry. One of its distinguishing features is the use of augmented reality (AR) to enhance learning by visually explaining complex coding concepts, making it easier for learners to grasp the material.
